Hello, my name is Jiale Zhang, a second-year Master of Science student in the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My major is Embedded Systems in the ECE department. I graduated from [ShanghaiTech University](https://www.shanghaitech.edu.cn/eng/) with a S.B. degree in the Electronic Information Engineering. 

My research interest focuses on the Combination of Human Computer Interaction and Embedded Systems. I am keen on developing novel human-oriented sensor systems to enable various methods of sensing and broaden the field of the perceived object. I am actively studying in the [ISC-Lab](https://theisclab.com/) under the instruction of [Alanson Sample](https://alansonsample.com/)

Besides doing awesome & crazy research, I am also a snowboarder, singer and hiker. These activities really help me relax in my spare time.
